# Enhanced Global Business Risk Assessment Tool
## Master Framework: 18-Domain Comprehensive Risk Analysis for 2026

---

## FRAMEWORK OVERVIEW

This enhanced framework integrates **two comprehensive risk analyses** (Global Business Risks Report + Global Strategic Risk Compendium) into a unified **18-domain assessment system** capturing the "polycrisis" operating environment of 2026.

### Architecture: 3-Tier Risk Categorization

**TIER 1: Strategic Macro Risks** (Foundation-level risks affecting all businesses)
- Strategic Risk (Geopolitics & Economic Nationalism)
- Financial Risk (Macroeconomic Instability)
- Compliance Risk (Regulatory Fragmentation)

**TIER 2: Operational & Functional Risks** (Business-execution risks)
- Operational Risk (Supply Chain & Climate)
- HR Risk (Demographic Squeeze & Burnout)
- Cybersecurity Risk (Digital Warfare)
- Investment Risk (Capital Markets & Unit Economics)

**TIER 3: Contextual & Stakeholder Risks** (Organizational culture & perception risks)
- Founder & Team Risk (Psychological & Interpersonal)
- Reputation Risk (Ethical Fallout & ESG Controversy)
- ESG Risk (Environmental & Biodiversity)
- Market & Product Risk (Product-Market Fit)
- Climate & Physical Risk (Extreme Weather & Infrastructure)
- Regulatory & Compliance (AI & Data Protection)
- External Risk (Litigation & Black Swan Events)

## INTELLIGENT SCORING SYSTEM (ENHANCED)

### Scoring Architecture

```
TIER 1 CALCULATION:
For each domain, administer 4-5 targeted questions

TIER 2 SCORING:
Domain Average = Mean(Q1-Q5) on Likert scale (1-5)
Inverse Risk Score = 100 - (Domain Average × 20)
Range: 0-100 (higher = greater risk exposure)

TIER 3 RISK FLAGGING:
Critical Flag (RED):      Any question ≤2 on high-impact category
Orange Flag (ORANGE):    2-3 questions ≤2, or domain average ≤2.5
Yellow Flag (YELLOW):    Mixed response pattern suggesting gaps

TIER 4 WEIGHTING & ADJUSTMENT:
Weighted Score = Σ(Domain Risk × Domain Weight)
Stage Multiplier: Pre-seed (1.4) → Seed (1.2) → Series A (1.0) → Series B+ (0.8)
Vertical Multiplier: Fintech (1.3) → Healthtech (1.2) → SaaS (1.0) → Hardware (1.1)
Polycrisis Multiplier: If 5+ domains scored >60, apply +15% penalty
```

### Risk Rating Scale

| **Score** | **Rating** | **Color** | **Implication** |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0-25 | MINIMAL | Green | Excellent risk management, mature controls |
| 26-40 | LOW | Light Green | Well-controlled, stage-appropriate gaps |
| 41-55 | MODERATE | Yellow | Requires active management, typical for stage |
| 56-70 | HIGH | Orange | Immediate action needed, threatens sustainability |
| 71-85 | CRITICAL | Red | Business viability compromised, urgent intervention required |
| 86-100 | EXISTENTIAL | Dark Red | Systemic failure likely without immediate restructuring |

## QUESTION ARCHITECTURE (72 Questions across 18 Domains)

### TIER 1: STRATEGIC & MACRO RISKS

#### Domain 1: Strategic Risk (4 Q's)
1. We have assessed and documented how our business aligns with government national security priorities in key markets
2. Our supply chain includes critical minerals, semiconductor components, or dual-use technologies subject to export controls
3. We have contingency plans if our primary markets implement discriminatory procurement or "national champion" requirements
4. We actively monitor geopolitical tensions affecting our sourcing regions and have diversified supplier strategies

#### Domain 2: Financial Risk (5 Q's)
5. Our business model and pricing are resilient to persistent inflation above 4% annually
6. We have modeled scenarios where interest rates remain "higher-for-longer" and assessed cash flow impact
7. Our revenue and valuation are not dependent on inflated AI-productivity expectations or speculative tech market sentiment
8. We maintain adequate working capital for 6+ months of operations at current burn rate
9. Our unit economics remain profitable even at elevated capital costs (8%+ WACC)

#### Domain 3: Compliance Risk: Tax & Regulatory (5 Q's)
10. We have audited our exposure to the 2025 TCJA expiration and tax cliff impacts (Section 199A deduction, rate changes)
11. Our business structure complies with the OECD's 15% global minimum tax (Pillar Two) requirements
12. We have processes to track and comply with "Making Tax Digital" mandates in UK and similar jurisdictions
13. We maintain clear documentation of "substance-based" business activities (payroll, tangible assets) for tax incentive safe harbors
14. We conduct annual compliance audits across all operating jurisdictions and have identified no material gaps

75. We monitor regulatory changes and litigation trends in our vertical; subscribe to compliance intelligence services

#### Domain 17: Cyber-Physical Risk (3 Q's)
76. All third-party vendors undergo security assessments before integration; cyber hygiene is contractually mandated

## ENHANCED SCORING ALGORITHM

### Step 1: Domain Score Calculation
For each domain:
```
domain_average = mean(question_responses_for_domain)
domain_risk_score = 100 - (domain_average × 20)
```
Result: 0-100 scale per domain

### Step 2: Risk Flag Detection
```
for each domain:
  if min(question_responses) <= 1:
    apply RED_FLAG (+50 points to overall score)
  elif min(question_responses) <= 2 AND count(responses <= 2) >= 2:
    apply ORANGE_FLAG (+30 points)
  elif domain_average <= 2.5:
    apply YELLOW_FLAG (+15 points)

critical_domains = [Founder Risk, Cybersecurity, AI Compliance]
for critical in critical_domains:
  if flag_detected:
    multiply_flag_penalty × 1.5
```

### Step 3: Composite Score with Weightings
```
weighted_score = Σ(domain_risk_score × domain_weight)

// Stage adjustment (accounts for maturity)
stage_multiplier = {
  'pre-seed': 1.4,
  'seed': 1.2,
  'series-a': 1.0,
  'series-b': 0.85,
  'series-c+': 0.7
}

// Vertical adjustment (accounts for regulatory intensity)
vertical_multiplier = {
  'fintech': 1.3,
  'healthtech': 1.25,
  'deeptech': 1.15,
  'saas-b2b': 1.0,
  'consumer': 0.9,
  'hardware': 1.1
}

// Polycrisis adjustment (if 5+ domains >60)
polycrisis_bonus = count(domains > 60) >= 5 ? 1.15 : 1.0

final_score = weighted_score × stage_multiplier × vertical_multiplier × polycrisis_bonus
final_score = min(final_score, 100)
```

### Step 4: Risk Profile & Recommendations
```
if final_score <= 25:
  rating = "MINIMAL"
  color = green
  action = "Maintain controls, annual review"
elif final_score <= 40:
  rating = "LOW"
  color = light_green
  action = "Monitor quarterly, address yellow flags"
elif final_score <= 55:
  rating = "MODERATE"
  color = yellow
  action = "Establish 90-day action plan for top 3 risks"
elif final_score <= 70:
  rating = "HIGH"
  color = orange
  action = "30-day emergency mitigation plan required"
elif final_score <= 85:
  rating = "CRITICAL"
  color = red
  action = "Immediate board-level intervention; restructuring required"
else:
  rating = "EXISTENTIAL"
  color = dark_red
  action = "Business model restructuring or pivot required"
```
